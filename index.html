About BattleConnect

BattleConnect is a mobile Android app for managing and visualizing IOBT (interet of battlefield things) data.

IOBT refers to battlefield devices that are connected in a similar manner to traditional IOT devices. With BattleConnect you can visualize IOBT sensors on the battlefield and get access to key IOBT data. BattleConnect also supports force tracking, allowing you to monitor the whereabouts of friendly and enemy forces. In addition to visualizing these elements, you can explore, search, and filter the data associated with these elements. BattleConnect also lets you receive important alerts from authorities and even lets you even submit reports directly from the battlefield. With BattleConnect in your hands, you have control of the situation.
